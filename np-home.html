One Community. One Purpose. Bridging the Gap.

Welcome to Faith and Recovery Network!

We are committed to connecting individuals with the resources, support, and community they need to thrive. Join us as we bridge gaps and empower lives of those suffering with mental health and substance abuse disorders, with the goal to help them foster understanding, reduce stigma, and promote healing.

Key Definitions

Term Definition Examples
Mental Health Disorder A condition that affects a person’s thinking, feeling, mood, or behavior. Depression, Anxiety Disorders, Bipolar Disorder
Substance Use Disorder A condition in which the recurrent use of alcohol or drugs causes significant impairment in health and daily life. Alcohol Dependence, Opioid Use Disorder
Health Equity Ensuring that everyone has a fair and just opportunity to achieve their highest level of health. Equal access to treatment, Removing barriers for underserved groups

Did You Know?

  • Substance Use Disorder (SUD) and Mental Health Disorders (MHD) impact not just the individual, but also families and communities.
  • About 1 in 5 adults in the U.S. experience a mental health condition each year.
  • Nearly 21 million people struggle with a Substance Use Disorder.
  • These challenges create a shared burden on loved ones, friends, and employers.
  • Support and understanding can help families and communities break the cycle and rebuild stronger, healthier connections.
